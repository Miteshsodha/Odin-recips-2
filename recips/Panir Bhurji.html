ðŸ““Ingredients:

  • For the Paneer Bhurji:
    • Paneer (Cottage Cheese): 250 grams, crumbled
    • Onion: 1 medium-sized, finely chopped
    • Tomato: 1 medium-sized, finely chopped
    • Green Chilies: 1-2, finely chopped (adjust to taste)
    • Ginger-Garlic Paste: 1 teaspoon
    • Turmeric Powder: 1/4 teaspoon
    • Red Chili Powder: 1/2 teaspoon (adjust to taste)
    • Garam Masala: 1/2 teaspoon
    • Cumin Seeds: 1/2 teaspoon
    • Fresh Coriander Leaves: a handful, finely chopped
    • Oil or Ghee: 2 tablespoons
    • Salt: to taste
    • Lemon Juice: 1 tablespoon (optional, for garnishing)
  • Optional ingredients:
    • Capsicum (Bell Pepper): 1 small, finely chopped
    • Green Peas: 1/4 cup, boiled (optional)
    • Butter: for extra richness

  • Heat the Oil or Ghee:
    • In a pan, heat 2 tablespoons of oil or ghee on medium flame.
  • Add Cumin Seeds:
    • Once the oil is hot, add 1/2 teaspoon of cumin seeds and let them splutter.
  • SautÃ© Onions:
    • Add 1 medium-sized finely chopped onion. SautÃ© until the onions turn golden brown.
  • Add Ginger-Garlic Paste:
    • Add 1 teaspoon of ginger-garlic paste and sautÃ© for another minute until the raw smell disappears.
  • Add Green Chilies:
    • Add 1-2 finely chopped green chilies (adjust to your spice preference) and sautÃ© for a few seconds.
  • Cook the Tomatoes:
    • Add 1 medium-sized finely chopped tomato. Cook until the tomatoes become soft and the oil starts to separate from the mixture.
  • Spice It Up:
    • Add 1/4 teaspoon of turmeric powder, 1/2 teaspoon of red chili powder, and 1/2 teaspoon of garam masala. Mix well and cook for another minute.
  • Add Capsicum (Optional):
    • If you're using capsicum (bell pepper), add 1 small finely chopped capsicum now. Cook for 2-3 minutes until the capsicum softens slightly.
  • Add Crumbled Paneer:
    • Add 250 grams of crumbled paneer to the pan. Mix everything well so that the paneer is coated with the spice mixture.
  • Add Green Peas (Optional):
    • If you're using green peas, add 1/4 cup of boiled peas at this stage. Mix well.
  • Cook the Paneer:
    • Cook for 3-4 minutes on medium-low heat, stirring occasionally. Ensure the paneer is well incorporated with the spices.
  • Check for Seasoning:
    • Add salt to taste and mix well. Adjust any spices if necessary.
  • Garnish:
    • Add a handful of finely chopped fresh coriander leaves and 1 tablespoon of lemon juice for a tangy flavor. Mix well.
  • Add Butter (Optional):
    • For extra richness, you can add a small piece of butter and let it melt into the bhurji.
  • Serve:
    • Serve hot with parathas, roti, bread, or as a filling for sandwiches or wraps.
